OER (Open Educational Resources)

Module : I.C.T

Author : Abdelmalek ELBOUDARY

Year : 2024

üìö Open Educational Resources (OER) are freely accessible educational materials that are available for use, reuse, modification, and sharing under an open license. These resources include textbooks, lecture notes, quizzes, videos, and other learning materials that are openly licensed to allow educators, students, and the general public to access and use them without cost or legal restrictions.

üåê OER are an integral part of the open education movement, which aims to make education more accessible, affordable, and equitable for learners worldwide. By removing barriers to access and allowing for the free exchange of knowledge, OER empower educators to adapt and customize learning materials to meet the diverse needs of their students and promote inclusive education.

üéì The benefits of OER extend beyond cost savings, fostering collaboration, innovation, and knowledge sharing among educators and learners. OER encourage the creation of high-quality, up-to-date educational resources that can be continuously improved and adapted to reflect advancements in teaching and learning practices. Moreover, OER support lifelong learning and enable individuals of all backgrounds to access educational opportunities and pursue their learning goals.
