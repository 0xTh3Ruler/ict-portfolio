SAMR categorizes technology integration into four levels, ranging from simple substitution to transformative redefinition, based on the extent to which technology enhances and transforms learning experiences.

Modification entails significant redesign of learning tasks or activities to leverage the capabilities of technology, while redefinition involves the creation of entirely new learning experiences that were previously inconceivable without technology.
