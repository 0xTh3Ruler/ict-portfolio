When sharing creative works online, it's essential to understand the conditions under which others can use, modify, and distribute them. Various licensing options exist to govern these conditions, providing creators with flexibility in how they want their works to be shared and used. These licenses, ranging from permissive to restrictive, offer different levels of freedom and protection to both creators and users. Below are seven common conditions of use licenses, each with its own terms and implications.
